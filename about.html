About Us

10+ Years Experience In Solar & Renewable Energy Industry

In the maximum efficiency division, we study the performance of all our panels throughout the product value chain, from the silicon extraction process to the treatment of the solar cells and their subsequent transformation into the TM-Series® solar panels that we offer our customers.

Solar Shakti is authorised distributor of APN Solar Energy Pvt Ltd. Leading provider of renewable solar energy solutions based in Mumbai. We deliver eco-friendly products to power homes, businesses, and communities with sustainable solar technology.

We have an partnership with “Contendre” Panels, which has a manufacturing factory in Bhiwandi (Maharashtra). Our venture, which is an MNRE-authorised manufacture, has the capacity to develop 70 MW of panels with high efficiency technology. The performance of the panels is A+.

High performance, quality, respect for the environment, and making clean energy profitable are our motivations to continue evolving in this industry.
The satisfaction of saving the environment and energy will make a large difference.

We provide End-to-End service for Goverment Subsidy

We provide AMC service for Solar Plant Maintanance Service

Solar Shakti has also developed a business franchise platform to provide quick service to end users, along with the supply of materials and installation services.
