Rooftop solar Installation Activities

Consumer

  • Submission of Solar Rooftop application in DISCOM portal
  • Selection of vendor from the list of empanelled vendors
  • Co-ordinate with vendor for system installation
  • Collect all the necessary documents from the installer
  • Signing Joint Inspection Report during plant commissioning

Vendor

  • Feasibility analysis of Solar Rooftop in consumer roof
  • Installation of Solar Rooftop plant as per the DISCOM specifications
  • Testing and commissioning of plant as per DISCOM guidelines
  • Signing Joint Inspection Report after plant commissioning
  • Undertaking operation and maintenance of project for 5 years post commissioning

DISCOM

  • Undertake technical feasibility of consumer application
  • Scrutinization of application documents
  • Testing, the Solar Rooftop Project and ensuring
    compliance of grid interconnection and safety standards.
  • Signing Joint Inspection Report after plant commissioning

About Us

APN Solar is a leading provider of renewable solar energy solutions based in Mumbai. We deliver eco-friendly products to power homes, businesses, and communities with sustainable solar technology. APN Solar Energy Pvt. Ltd., also known as Alternative Power Network, is a JV manufacturer, energy consultant, and engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) company for residential, commercial, and industrial power. We understand our customers energy consumption patterns and make it economically viable to switch to renewable energy sources. APN was established in 2017 in Mumbai and has expanded its network to all major cities.

APN Solar has a JV Manufacture unit along with quality product panels to deliver up to 2 GWPA.

APN has also developed a business franchise platform to provide quick service to end users, along with the supply of materials and installation services.



WHO WE ARE ?

APN Solar is a leading solar energy company, driven by a team of passionate professionals who share a common vision of a greener and more sustainable planet. Our Product Execution Process

Step 1 (Site Visit)

We make sure our client gets all the information on the very first day of our visit with the POA of our work.

Step 2 (Design and Documentation)

Making a clear view about the way the whole project will look like & PPA for Net metering Process.

Step 3 (Fabrication & Installation)

A complete frame of GI structure with bolts & nuts for better strength & Installation according to the Single Line Diagram (SLD) with proper chain of Strings & Series.

Step 4 (Commissioning & Net Meter)

Final step when the entire solar system ready to connect with Net Meter along with PPA.
